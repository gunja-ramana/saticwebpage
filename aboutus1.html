Welcome To Our Company

A sari consists of one very long piece of fabric that's wrapped around the body and draped over a shoulder, and garments worn underneath, including a short-sleeved blouse. The style and fabric of a sari, as well as the way it's worn, varies between different regions and traditions. You can also spell this word saree. The word "sari" comes from the Sanskrit word śāṭikā and the first mention of saris is in the Rig Veda, a Hindu book of hymns dating back to 3,000 B.C. The history of sari-like clothing can be traced back to the Indus Valley Civilization.
